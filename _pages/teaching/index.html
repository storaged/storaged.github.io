Let's take Your English and German classes to a new amazing level

Are you ready to embark on a journey of language learning? Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, I am here to guide you every step of the way. As a dedicated English and German teacher, I offer personalized lessons tailored to your needs and goals. With proficiency ranging from A1 to C1 levels, you'll find the right level of instruction to suit your current abilities.

Whether you're aiming for work, travel, academic pursuits, or certifications, my classes are designed to help you achieve your objectives.

All classes are conducted fully online via Zoom, ensuring a convenient and flexible learning experience. This means you can learn from the comfort of your own space, without the need to commute.

Complete Beginners (A1):

  • Build a Solid Foundation: Learn essential vocabulary and grammar to start basic conversations.
  • Greetings and Introductions: Master introducing yourself and exchanging pleasantries confidently.
  • Everyday Situations: Navigate common situations like shopping, ordering at a restaurant, and asking for directions.

Intermediate Learners (A2-B1):

  • Expanded Vocabulary: Enhance your language skills by learning more diverse vocabulary and expressions.
  • Conversational Skills: Engage in discussions on various topics with increased fluency.
  • Reading and Writing: Comprehend simple texts and express yourself in written form.

Advanced Learners (B2-C1):

  • Fluency Development: Fine-tune your language skills for natural and articulate conversations.
  • Cultural Insights: Deepen your understanding of English and German cultures through language nuances.
  • Business or Academic Language: Specialize your language abilities for professional or academic contexts.
