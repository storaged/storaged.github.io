professional interests

Committed to transforming education through innovative and engaging methods, I am passionate about infusing life into challenging subjects like grammar, seeking the most effective ways to inspire and teach students. Constantly expanding my skills, I am currently learning French to broaden my future teaching opportunities.

current work

My current work as an online English and German Teacher covers:

  • Catering to a diverse range of over ten regular students, I provide comprehensive language instruction spanning from A1 to C1 levels.
  • Offering exam preparation assistance, I guide students in honing their language skills while preparing for assessments.
  • Conducting conversational sessions in both English and German, I leverage my experience working with corporate clients, particularly in the IT industry, to deliver tailored language classes.
